How the Process Works

From first contact to system activation, here is what to expect when you work with us.

1

Free Estimate

We collect your electricity bill details and roof information and build a site-specific production and savings model. No obligation, no sales pressure.

2

Proposal Review

You receive a transparent proposal with system size, projected production, annual savings, financing options, and break-even timeline, all explained line by line.

3

Permits and CEIP

We handle building permit submission and, where applicable, CEIP application documentation. You don't need to navigate municipal processes yourself.

4

Installation

Our crews install the system, typically in one to two days for a standard rooftop installation. All electrical work is performed by licensed electricians.

5

Interconnection

We coordinate with your utility for net billing interconnection approval and activation. You start receiving credits for exported power as soon as the utility completes their process.

6

Ongoing Monitoring

Your system includes production monitoring so you can verify output against projections. We're available for any performance questions after activation.
