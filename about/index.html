About Alberta Solar Advisors

We help Alberta homeowners understand the real numbers behind solar before they commit to anything. Transparent proposals, no sales pressure.

Who We Are

Alberta Solar Advisors was established in 2020 to fill a gap in the Alberta solar market: homeowners were being given vague savings claims and pressure-sales tactics instead of transparent information.

Our model is different. We walk you through an honest financial analysis: what the system costs, what it will produce based on your specific roof, what your electricity savings look like year by year, and what the payback period is. You get a real proposal, not a sales pitch.

When you're ready to move forward, we match you with Orizon Energy, our trusted installation partner serving Alberta since 2019. Orizon handles permitting, installation, inspections, and utility interconnection. Alberta Solar Advisors handles the education and proposal stage so you arrive at the conversation already informed.

What We Believe About Solar

Transparency builds trust

We publish real case studies with actual bills and real production data. Our proposals show break-even timelines, not just savings highlights. If solar doesn't pencil out for your home, we'll tell you.

Alberta has a real solar case

With some of Canada's highest electricity rates, a strong net billing framework, and CEIP financing in key cities, the economics of Alberta solar are genuinely compelling for most homes consuming over 10,000 kWh per year.

Education comes before the sale

We created our bill breakdown case studies, financing comparisons, and incentive guides so homeowners arrive at the conversation already understanding the basics. An informed homeowner makes a better decision.

The installer matters

Solar is a 25-year asset. The company that installs it needs to be around in year 10 when you have a question or need a warranty claim handled. We partner exclusively with Orizon Energy because of their track record and local service commitment.

Our Service Area

We serve homeowners across Alberta, with primary operations based in Edmonton. Our team has completed installations in Edmonton, Calgary, Red Deer, Lethbridge, Airdrie, St. Albert, Sherwood Park, Spruce Grove, Medicine Hat, Grande Prairie, Fort McMurray, and Slave Lake.
