Our mission: transparency first

The solar industry has a reputation problem. Too many homeowners have been sold oversized systems, given inflated savings projections, or locked into financing products that don't serve their interests. We started Alberta Solar Advisors because we believe the province deserves better.

We're a local community resource built around one core principle: give homeowners honest, fact-checked information so they can make informed decisions. If solar doesn't make financial sense for your home, we'll tell you , and explain why. If it does, we'll connect you with licensed, vetted installers and help you navigate every incentive program available.

We're operated by Simple Tree Digital under the Orizon Energy umbrella. Our network has helped coordinate solar installations for over 1,355 Alberta homes and businesses, and our case studies reflect real outcomes , not cherry-picked best-case scenarios.

What drives us: love for Alberta

Alberta is an extraordinary province. From the Rockies to the prairies, from the oil sands to the coulees of the south , this is a place that knows how to produce energy. For over a century, Alberta has powered Canada. We believe the next chapter of that story runs on sunshine, and Alberta's homeowners deserve to be part of it.

Alberta has more sunshine than any other major Canadian province. Calgary averages more sunshine hours annually than Miami. Edmonton's summer days last over 17 hours. The economics of solar here , particularly with retail-rate net billing and programs like the CEIP and Solar Club™ , are among the strongest in North America.

We also understand that Albertans value independence. Owning your own energy system, generating your own power, and reducing what you owe your utility every month , that's a very Alberta idea. Solar is fundamentally about ownership and self-reliance, and those are values that resonate deeply in this province.

Our goal is to make sure every Alberta homeowner who wants to go solar has access to the same quality of information and advice, regardless of where they live or who they know. That's why we publish detailed case studies, keep our incentive tracking up to date, and make our estimates free with no obligation.
