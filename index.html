Alberta Solar Incentives

Programs that make solar affordable now

The federal Greener Homes Loan has ended. These programs are active and available to qualifying Alberta homeowners today.

CEIP Municipal Financing

Finance your system through your property tax with zero upfront cost. Available in Edmonton, Airdrie, Lethbridge, St. Albert, and more. Calgary is expected to reopen in 2026.

Learn about CEIP →

Net Billing (Micro-Generation)

Alberta's Micro-Generation Regulation lets you earn retail rate credits for every kilowatt-hour you export back to the grid, rolling over for 12 months.

How net billing works →

The Solar Club Rates

Solar Club members can switch between a $0.30/kWh high rate in summer and a $0.0877/kWh low rate in winter, optimizing credits and dramatically reducing winter bills.
