Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Alberta Solar Advisors, operated by Orizon Energy Inc. and Simple Tree Digital, is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website and customers who engage with our services. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you submit our solar estimate form, contact us by phone or email, or book an appointment. This includes your name, email address, phone number, home address, and information about your property and electricity usage.

We also collect information automatically when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and duration of visits, referring URLs, and device information. We use Google Analytics (GA4), Google Tag Manager, and Meta Pixel to collect this data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To prepare and deliver your free solar estimate
  • To contact you about your inquiry and schedule consultations
  • To process applications for CEIP financing on your behalf
  • To manage our relationship with you if you become a customer
  • To improve our website and marketing programs
  • To comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information with Orizon Energy Inc. as the solar installation partner for proposals and installations you request. We may also share information with service providers who assist us with CRM, marketing, and analytics, including GoHighLevel, Google, and Meta, under agreements that require them to protect your information.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to operate and improve. These include analytics cookies (Google Analytics), advertising measurement cookies (Google Ads, Meta Pixel), and functional cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Your Rights Under PIPEDA

As a Canadian resident, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you, request corrections to inaccurate information, withdraw consent to certain uses, and request deletion of your personal information subject to legal requirements. To exercise these rights, contact us at Info@mail.solar-resource.org.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. For customers, this is typically the duration of the service relationship plus 7 years for financial records.
